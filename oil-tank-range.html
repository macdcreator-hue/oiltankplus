Regulations

When Is a Bunded Tank Required?

UK regulations (Water Industry Act and associated guidance) require a bunded tank in these circumstances:

  • Within 50 metres of a watercourse, stream, river or drain
  • Within a flood risk zone (Environment Agency flood maps)
  • On commercial or agricultural premises of any size
  • Domestic tanks holding more than 3,500 litres
