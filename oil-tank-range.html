Bunded Oil Tanks

Also known as double-skinned tanks, bunded tanks have an inner tank fully contained within an outer tank. If the inner tank leaks, the outer skin contains the oil — providing an essential layer of environmental protection.

Bunded tanks are required by law in many situations — including properties near watercourses, within flood zones, or on commercial premises. Even where not legally required, they're widely considered best practice.
