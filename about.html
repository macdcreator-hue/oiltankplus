Bunded tanks are legally required in certain situations: within 50m of a watercourse, within a flood risk zone, on commercial premises, or if your tank holds more than 3,500 litres. In all other cases they're not legally required for domestic use, but they are strongly recommended for the added environmental protection. Our engineers will advise which is most appropriate for your property.
Most domestic tank installations are completed within half a day. This includes removal of your old tank, siting the new tank, making all pipe connections, and commissioning your system. You'll receive your OFTEC certificate on the day. More complex installations or commercial jobs may take longer — your engineer will give you a clear timeframe at the survey stage.
