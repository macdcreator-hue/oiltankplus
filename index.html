In most cases, no. Replacing a domestic oil tank like-for-like is generally considered permitted development under UK planning law. However, if you're installing for the first time, significantly increasing capacity, or the property is listed or in a conservation area, you may need to apply. Our engineers will advise during your free site survey.
Bunded tanks are legally required in certain situations: within 50m of a watercourse, within a flood risk zone, on commercial premises, or for tanks holding more than 3,500 litres. For all other domestic installations they are not legally required — but they are strongly recommended for the added environmental protection. We'll advise which is right for your property.
Our installation price includes supply of the new tank, removal and responsible disposal of your old tank, all pipework and connections, commissioning your heating system, and your OFTEC certificate. There are no hidden extras — you'll receive a transparent, fixed-price quote before any work begins.
Most domestic installations are completed within half a day. This includes removal of your old tank, siting the new tank, making all pipe connections and commissioning. More complex installations or commercial projects may take longer — your engineer will give you a clear timeframe at the survey stage.
Yes — every installation we carry out is completed by an OFTEC-registered engineer, and you receive your OFTEC certificate on installation day. This certificate is a legal requirement and confirms the work has been carried out to the correct standard. You'll need it for insurance purposes and when you sell the property.
We remove and responsibly recycle your old tank as part of the standard installation service at no extra cost. It's pumped out, cleaned and taken to our approved recycling partner under a zero-landfill policy. You receive a waste transfer note confirming legal, environmentally responsible disposal.
Yes — we offer standalone tank disposal as a separate service. Whether you're decommissioning a property, switching to a different fuel type, or simply removing an unused tank, we handle the whole process safely and legally. Contact us for a disposal-only quote.
